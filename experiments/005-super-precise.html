005 Superprecise

Notes

A five-round precision game. A scanner sweeps across a 0–100 track and you have to stop it on a target value to two decimal places. Each round the scanner moves faster.

Scoring is exponential: a delta of 0.00 awards the full 1000 points, 0.50 awards roughly 600, 5.00 awards near zero. The best run ever is kept locally.
