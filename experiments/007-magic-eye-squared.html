007 Magic Eye²

Notes

An autostereogram built on the canvas: a horizontally-tiled random pattern with each pixel shifted by a depth map of an eye, so a divergent or crossed gaze fuses adjacent tiles into a 3D form.

The pattern is regenerated from a seed; the depth map stays put. The two coral fiducials at the top help calibrate the gaze — when you can fuse them into three dots, the image will click.
