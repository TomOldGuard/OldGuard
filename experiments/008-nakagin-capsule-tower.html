008 Nakagin Capsule Tower

Notes

Kisho Kurokawa's Nakagin Capsule Tower (Tokyo, 1972) was the built manifesto of Metabolism: 140 identical prefabricated capsules bolted onto two concrete cores, each meant to be unplugged and swapped as the city changed. None ever were. The tower was dismantled in 2022.

A sandbox in that spirit — no puzzle, no goal. Every module is the same capsule, same round porthole. Drag one from the rack onto the site, or tap the grid to drop a capsule and stack towers. Switch to Remove to peel modules back off. Build whatever city you like; it is kept locally between visits.
