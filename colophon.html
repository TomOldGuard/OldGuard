01 About this site

Old Guard is a digital sandbox — a public archive of experiments at the intersection of AI and design.

Each experiment is a contained study: a generative artefact, an interactive demo, a motion test, a small game or a piece of typographic mischief.

Some are useful. Some are not.

02 Authorship

Made by Tom Johnson collaboratively with Claude.
