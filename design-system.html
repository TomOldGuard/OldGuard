00 Design system

The whole system in one place, rendered live — the foundations (colour, type, layout) and the component primitives that build every experiment. styles.css owns the page chrome and tokens; components.css owns the shared .og-* layer. Compose experiments from these and write bespoke CSS only for the part that makes a demo unique. If anything here looks off, the whole index drifts — so this page is also the drift check.

01 Colour
Six tokens, defined in :root and flipped wholesale by [data-theme]. Toggle the half-circle in the header to watch them invert. Coral is the one constant across both themes.

10 Motion & voice
The connective character of the system beyond what you can swatch — how things move and how they read.
Motion

Subtle hover states only — 120–200ms transitions, a small offset shift, an accent reveal. Hover any button above to feel it. Real movement lives in the experiment demos, not the chrome.

Voice

Minimal, experimental, design-forward, playful, precise. It belongs on the internet of ideas, not a template. Avoid AI-slop tropes — gradients, rounded-corner-plus-left-accent cards, stray emoji.
